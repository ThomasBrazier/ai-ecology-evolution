Events

La prochaine conférence LEGEND en machine learning pour la génomique évolutive aura lieu à Aussois du 8 au 12 décembre 2025. Les keynotes seront:

  • Anne-Florence Bitbol (EPFL): Coevolution-aware language models
  • laudia Solís-Lemus (University of Wisconsin-Madison): The good, the bad and the ugly of deep learning in phylogenetic inference
  • Burak Yelmen: A perspective on generative neural networks in genomics with applications in synthetic data generation

Toutes les informations pratiques sont sur le site de la conférence: https://legend2025.sciencesconf.org/

Packages
  • mlverse/torch A R package to use the Torch deep learning framework natively in R.
  • mlverse/luz A R package to train and evaluate torch neural networks.
  • keras3.posit.co A R package to develop with the Keras high-level neural networks API. Maybe less flexible than mlverse/torch for experimentation, but a robust framework.
  • caret A R package with miscellaneous functions for training and plotting classification and regression models. On github.
  • rGBIF A R package for Open Data on species occurence from the GBIF database.
